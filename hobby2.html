How much do I read?

The most books I've read in one year is about 60 books. Now that I am in college, I find myself reading less recreationally because of my workload but it still something I enjoy as a past time.

What do I like about reading?

Reading gives me an opportunity to enjoy some quiet time, either to learn something new or to escape to an imaginary world. I think reading is valuable because it has the potential to expand one's creative mind and it is also proven to improve writing skills.
